# Huckleberry Notes

- Sleep authority + all-in-one parenting tracker.
- Bundles AI guidance (Berry) as Premium differentiator.

## UX and Visual Signals
- Utility-first dashboard with predictive timing patterns.
- Emphasis on confidence through routines and insights.

## Strengths Observed
- Strong sleep timing proposition and caregiver sync messaging.
- Clear free/plus/premium ladder with AI tied to top tier.

## Potential Weaknesses
- Brand and product weight concentrated on sleep; less obvious postpartum wellness depth in sampled artifacts.

## Blueberry Opportunity Angle
- Offer stage-continuous guidance across pregnancy and postpartum with stronger partner-aware context summaries.
