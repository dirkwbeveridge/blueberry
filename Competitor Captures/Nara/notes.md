# Nara Notes

## Positioning Snapshot
- Free, ad-free, calm design for baby + pregnancy + postpartum continuity.
- Emphasis on intuitive usage and caregiver coordination.

## UX and Visual Signals
- Minimal, soft visual language with low cognitive load.
- Strong emotional and routine continuity across mom and baby tracking.

## Strengths Observed
- High customer affinity for simplicity and visual calm.
- Postpartum and mom self-tracking represented in one flow.

## Potential Weaknesses
- Pricing/monetization leverage appears less explicit in sampled artifacts.

## Blueberry Opportunity Angle
- Keep calm visual strategy but add premium-grade contextual intelligence and partnership-specific handoff summaries.
