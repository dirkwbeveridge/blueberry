# Sprout Baby Tracker Notes

## Positioning Snapshot
- Comprehensive newborn tracking utility with strong logging depth.
- Family sharing and on-the-go logging emphasized.

## UX and Visual Signals
- Data-rich, tracker-first layout priorities.
- Strong operational utility framing.

## Strengths Observed
- Detailed feed/sleep/diaper/pumping and trend views.
- Clear practical value for multi-caregiver coordination.

## Potential Weaknesses
- Review friction around tracking precision defaults and certain workflow details.

## Blueberry Opportunity Angle
- Keep utility depth while reducing logging friction through AI-assisted structured entry and better quick-edit flows.
