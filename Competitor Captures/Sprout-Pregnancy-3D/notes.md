# Sprout Pregnancy Tracker 3D Notes

## Positioning Snapshot
- 3D fetal development and informational pregnancy journey.
- Feature-rich tracking with journal/checklist support.

## UX and Visual Signals
- Educational and content-heavy layout style.
- High visual novelty via 3D growth presentation.

## Strengths Observed
- Strong pregnancy education cues and visual storytelling.
- Broad tracking utilities and watch/widget references.

## Potential Weaknesses
- User feedback includes desire for less directive advice and more neutral/scientific framing.

## Blueberry Opportunity Angle
- Deliver evidence-aware guidance language with user-controlled tone (reassuring vs factual) and cleaner prioritization of what matters today.
