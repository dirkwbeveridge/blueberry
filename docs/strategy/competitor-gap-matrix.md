## Synthesis: Top Build Opportunities

1. AI assistant with household and stage context, monetized in premium tiers.
2. Postpartum-first continuity across mom health, baby health, and partner support.
3. Low-friction partner handoff summaries and timeline reconciliation.
4. Calm UI system that preserves utility depth while reducing cognitive load.
5. Faster capture paths (structured quick logs + AI assist) instead of longer form entry.
