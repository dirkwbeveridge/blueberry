What Blueberry Is

Private two-user pregnancy companion app

A household mobile app for exactly two users: Mother and Partner. Not a public product. Both users share one household view from separate phones — pregnancy progress, shared todos, health logs, journal entries, appointments, kick counting, and labor timing.

Expo Router TypeScript strict Supabase (Auth + Postgres + Realtime) Zustand Playfair Display + DM Sans Plum / Ivory palette

Tab Capabilities (Locked)

Tab Who Capabilities
Home Both Pregnancy stage, weeks, trimester progress, baby development this week, next appointment, Today’s Focus, quick actions (Add Note, Share Update)
Health Mom only Symptom and wellness logs (body, energy, mindfulness, sleep), health history, Apple Health connector placeholder, data insight visualizations, Add Question for Provider
Together Partner only Support mode, what she needs today (based on current stage/shared updates), shared check-ins, household handoff, conversation prompts, support idea generator, Add Note, Share Update
To Do Both Task + Calendar + Appointment control center. Create/update/delete/close todos and appointments. Categories: owner, must-do/nice-to-do, due date, notes. Two dashboards: Todos (upcoming/priority) and Appointments (next visit, question log, notes, prep). Google Calendar sync placeholder.
Memories Both Journal, photos, milestones, little notes without daily obligation. Recent moments, Share update with partner.
More Both Everything else: partner support (if Mom), household settings, appointments overflow, future postpartum tools. Inactive role-specific tab reachable here only. Mom health logs are not exposed to Partner via More.

Realtime sync + push notifications planned for: new todos (partner), new appointments (both), new shared update/check-in (both). These are Phase 7 scope.
